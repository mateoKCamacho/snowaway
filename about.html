About SnowAway

We’re two university students from Kingston who started SnowAway to make winter income while helping neighbours stay safe and snow-free. No franchise, no call centre — just the two of us, shovels in hand.

  • What we do: Clear driveways, walkways, and front steps — with salting included.
  • When we come: We head out once ~4 cm of snow has accumulated. Precise timing can be hard to predict, but after overnight snow your driveway and paths are generally cleared before 8:00 am.
  • Where: Kingston and nearby neighbourhoods so we can respond quickly in storms.
