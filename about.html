About SnowAway

We’re two university students from Kingston. We started SnowAway to earn winter income while helping neighbours stay safe and snow-free. No franchise, no call center — just the two of us, shovels in hand.

  • What we do: Driveways, walkways, front steps. Light salting on request.
  • Where: Kingston and nearby neighbourhoods for fast response.
  • When: Early mornings, evenings, and during storms around class schedules.
