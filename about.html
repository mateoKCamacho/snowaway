About SnowAway

We’re two university students from Kingston who started SnowAway to make some money in the winter while helping neighbours stay safe and snow-free. No franchise, no call center — just the two of us, shovels in hand.

  • What we do: Shovel driveways, walkways, and front steps. Light salting on request.
  • Where we work: Kingston and nearby neighbourhoods only (to keep response times fast).
  • When: Early mornings, evenings, and during storms — we work around class schedules.
